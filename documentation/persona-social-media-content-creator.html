User Flow

  1. They open up the website on their mac
  2. they are brought to the home page
  3. they scroll across the navbar and notice the name and the "moods" categories tab
  4. they hover over the moods with their mouse
  5. THEN the button has a pop up text box describing that moods are how you can adjust the content from the quote generator
  6. they press the button
  7. THEN a list of possible moods expands from it at the right corner of the screen
  8. they select a mood option
  9. THEN the background of the screen changes color and image to reflect that mood
  10. they hover their mouse over the quotes generator input bar next to it is a randomizer button
  11. They choose the randomizer button
  12. THEN the link in the button takes to a new page with the background of the page a random/contextual meme image and a quote. beneath the quote almost to the bottom of the screen is a button "download/save to profile"
  13. They arent sure they want to make a profile but they press the button to download the image
  14. THEN the button produces a popout modal that has 2 buttons inside "download" and "create profile(replaced by "save to personal gallery" for member users)"
  15. they click "download"
  16. THEN the page opens the downloader and the png file with the quote over-layed on the image is saved to their download folder
  17. They decide to get a few more quote/images
  18. THEN after the 3rd image the downloader modal shows a new text "to download more create a profile!"
  19. THEN they are no longer able to download or copy the image
  20. Still needing more images they press the create profile button
  21. THEN they are taken to a new page called "profile creation" it asks them a series of information questions in input bar sections
  22. They complete the empty input bars and click the button at the bottom of the screen marked "complete"
  23. THEN they are taken to the profile page
  24. They scan about the page and see the gallery with their previous 3
  25. They click on one of their previous image/quotes
  26. Then the page reverts back to the image background page with the quote and download button near the bottom of the screen
  27. They download a few more images and bookmark the site for later
