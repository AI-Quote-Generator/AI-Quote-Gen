The Moodser

Name: 30

Age: Debbie Downer

Profession: Customer Service Representative at a retail store

Technology

Debbie is proficient in using Windows 11 Pro on her Dell OptiPlex to complete work tasks. When she's at home, she switches to her Acer laptop, which runs Windows 11 Home to watch YouTube videos, browse social media, and send emails. Additionally, she uses her iPhone 13, which runs iOS 16.3, for various personal tasks

Attitudes and Behaviors

As a customer services representative at a retail store, Debbie has a positive attitude and tries to remain professional and courteous when interacting with customers. However, most of her day is spent dealing with frustrated customers and trying to resolve their issues. Although her job can be difficult at times, she takes pride in being able to help people and provide good customer service.

Frustration and Needs

Her job is stressful and de-motivational, as she often deals with angry or difficult people. This job can also be emotionally draining, and she finds herself needing a mood boost throughout the day. She would need easy access to funny and sarcastic content that can provide that mood boost, even when dealing with difficult customers.

User-Story

When I'm having a tough day at work, I like to watch funny and sarcastic content as a pick-me-up to help me find humor in my stressful job and boost my mood.

Use Case

Debbie's mood boost during a busy day at work. She will be using the website on her work computer or phone during her break to boost her mood and help her find humor in her stressful job during a busy day at work.

Interaction-Flow

  1. Debbie logs onto her computer or mobile device and opens her web browser.
  2. She navigates to the website
  3. On the website's homepage, she searches the mood tab at the top of the website's navbar
  4. A drop-box appears and a list of moods is listed
  5. She scrolls down the list to find funny and sarcastic quotes related to customer service and clicks on the "generate-quote" button
  6. A larger image appears with a card section to the top right corner of the post and then a quote is generated over the image.
  7. She chuckles at the meme, then presses the favorite icon located within the card section in the top right corner of the post.
  8. The favorite icon changes color to indicate that the post has been liked and added to favorites
  9. She continues scrolling and sees a quote that she finds particularly funny and decides to share it wither her colleagues.
  10. She presses on the paper airplane icon that is also located in the card section in the top right corner of the post
  11. a pop-up window appears with a list of contacts with a send button next to each contact.
  12. After she clicks on the send button, the send icon changes color indicating the post was shared.
  13. She backs out of the pop-up window and continues scrolling.
  14. She finds another funny post and decides she wants to leave a comment relating to the post.
  15. She clicks on the comment icon located in the same card section in the top right corner of the post.
  16. Another pop-up window appears with other comments from other users.
  17. A text area appears under all the comments where she can type her comment.
  18. After typing her comment in the text area, she clicks on the submit button and her comment gets added to the list of comments in the comment section.
  19. Debbie backs out of the comment section and continues to browse the website's content by clicking the gallery button.
  20. She scrolls through the gallery to find more funny and sarcastic memes.
  21. After spending a few minutes on the website, she feels re-energized and ready to tackle the rest of her day with renewed motivation and a positive attitude.
  22. Debbie closes the website and resumes her work tasks.
