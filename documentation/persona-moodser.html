The Moodser

Name: 30

Age: Debbie Downer

Profession: Customer Service Representative at a retail store

Technology

Debbie is proficient in using Windows 11 Pro on her Dell OptiPlex to complete work tasks. When she's at home, she switches to her Acer laptop, which runs Windows 11 Home to watch YouTube videos, browse social media, and send emails. Additionally, she uses her iPhone 13, which runs iOS 16.3, for various personal tasks

Attitudes and Behaviors

As a customer services representative at a retail store, Debbie has a positive attitude and tries to remain professional and courteous when interacting with customers. However, most of her day is spent dealing with frustrated customers and trying to resolve their issues. Although her job can be difficult at times, she takes pride in being able to help people and provide good customer service.

Frustration and Needs

Her job is stressful and de-motivational, as she often deals with angry or difficult people. This job can also be emotionally draining, and she finds herself needing a mood boost throughout the day. She would need easy access to funny and sarcastic content that can provide that mood boost, even when dealing with difficult customers.

User-Story

When I'm having a tough day at work, I like to watch funny and sarcastic content as a pick-me-up to help me find humor in my stressful job nd boost my mood.

Use Case

Debbie's mood boost during a busy day at work. She will be using the website on her work computer or phone during her break to boost her mood and help her find humor in her stressful job during a busy dat at work.

Interaction-Flow

  1. Debbie logs onto her computer or mobile device and opens her web browser.
  2. She navigates to the website
  3. On the website's homepage, she searches the mood tab at the top of the website's navbar
  4. A drop-box appears and a list of moods is listed
  5. She looks for funny and sarcastic quotes, such as memes and jokes related to customer service.
  6. She clicks on the "generate-quote" button, which opens up a larger image and generates the quote.
  7. She chuckles at the meme
  8. She then likes/hearts it and scrolls down to view more content.
  9. She sees a quote that she finds particularly funny and decides to share it wither her colleagues.
  10. She leaves a comment relating to the post.
  11. Then she clicks the "share" button and sends the quote to her colleagues via email or instant message.
  12. Debbie continues to browse the website's content by clicking the gallery button.
  13. Goes through the gallery to find more funny and sarcastic memes.
  14. After spending a few minutes on the website, she feels re-energized and ready t o tackle the rest of her day with renewed motivation and a positive attitude.
  15. Debbie closes the website and resumes her work tasks.
